Precio en demanda



  

Set in the iconic location of Porto Cervo, this luxury private villa in Sardinia is one of the ultimate estates available for weekly rentals when it comes to a glamorous Italian vacation. Abaco is a real modern architectural gem, boasting particular design features such as the harmonious fusion between the use of stone and Mediterranean vegetation in the ambiance. This elegant two-story property includes six polished bedrooms provided with en suite bathrooms. With separate entrances to each room, this villa is ideal for friends and couples travelling together. In addition, there are four more bedrooms dedicated to the villa staff on the lower ground floor.
The minimal interiors are marvellously brightened by the sunlight through floor-to-ceiling windows to make sea lovers feel just a few steps away from the Smeralda Coast.
Besides the two floors, the luxury property includes a vast terrace with a terrific view over the maritime landscape. Up here, the whole roof is completely covered by lawn to be used as a solarium area. Abaco comes with an added value: a majestic multi-level swimming pool where guests can treat themselves to long swims and the pleasure of a Jacuzzi massage accompanied by astonishing sceneries.
